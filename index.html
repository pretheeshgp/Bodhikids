Fascilitating body-being abilities for children.
We fascilitate not only neccesary capaicty to acuqire practical skills of life but also help them to find joy in their moving alive body. We fasciliate art of meeting difficulties or challenges with love and bodily joy. They will be given the taste and real life experience of acquiring and developing skills with love and not through mere competetive based negativities and fears which is the defualt in our society.
We Are Bodhikids And Childhood Is Our Passion.
We are a team focused on not letting any child move into adulthood without noticing and realizing any hidden talents and also let them get in touch with their unique talent and explore them on a daily basis apart from basic body-being exercises which are simply their bodily birth-right. In short we let them touch with their curios healthy playful self and find their own joyous being.
